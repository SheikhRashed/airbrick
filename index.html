Rent-to-Rent Explained!

Also known as Rent-to-Serviced Accommodation in the UK or Rental Arbitrage in the USA. Rent-to-Rent is where Airbrick Finance rents a property from a landlord on a 3 to 5 years lease and pays a fixed and guaranteed rent, while short letting the property on Airbnb. This is one of the most profitable property strategies in the UK. It's compliant with UK law and can generate a substantial earning potential for investors.
